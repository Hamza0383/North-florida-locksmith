About North Florida Locksmith

Locally owned, owner-operated, and built on the belief that a locksmith should be honest about what you need — not just what's most profitable to sell you.

Call Us Anytime — We Always Answer

Who We Are

North Florida Locksmith is an independently owned and operated locksmith company serving Jacksonville and the surrounding communities of Northeast Florida. We're not a franchise, not a call center, and not a national brand with a local phone number that forwards to a dispatch center in another state. Every call goes directly to our team, and every technician who shows up at your door is someone who works for us.

We started this business because we saw a real problem in this market: too many locksmith companies operating with deceptive pricing, fake local listings, and contractors who weren't trained or accountable to anyone. Homeowners and business owners in North Florida deserved better — a real local option with transparent pricing and technicians who actually knew what they were doing.

That's what we built. And it's what we show up to do every day, whether it's a simple house lockout at noon or a broken commercial lock at 3 a.m.

We cover a wide stretch of Northeast Florida, including all of Duval, St. Johns, Clay, and Nassau Counties. Our mobile technicians are positioned across the region so response times stay realistic — not just good-sounding numbers on a website.

What Sets Us Apart

We Quote Before We Work

Every job starts with a quote you approve before a single bolt is turned. The price we give you on the phone is the price you pay. No trip fees added after arrival. No weekend surcharges slipped in at the end. No "emergency rate" disclosed once we're already standing at your door. If the scope of a job changes once we're on-site, we stop, explain why, and get your approval before continuing.

Owner-Operated — Real Accountability

When you call a national locksmith chain or an aggregator service, your job often gets subcontracted to a third-party tech you know nothing about. That's not how we operate. Our technicians work directly for us. That means they're trained our way, vetted by us, and accountable to us — and by extension, to you.

Licensed and Verifiable in Florida

Florida regulates locksmiths through the Department of Business & Professional Regulation (DBPR). We hold the required state licensing and encourage you to verify any locksmith you're considering — including us. If a locksmith can't provide a Florida DBPR license number when asked, that's a serious red flag.

Honest Recommendations

We'll tell you if rekeying is the right call instead of a full lock replacement — even when the lock replacement would cost you more. We'll tell you if your existing hardware is in good shape and doesn't need to be upgraded. We'd rather give you honest advice and have you call us again in the future than oversell you today.

24/7 Availability — Real People Answer

Our emergency line is answered by a real person, around the clock, every day of the year including holidays. You won't be routed to a voicemail, an answering service that can't dispatch anyone, or a callback form. When you have a lockout or a security emergency, you reach someone who can actually help.

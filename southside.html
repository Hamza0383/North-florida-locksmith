Do you make car keys on site in Southside?

Yes. Our mobile units carry the equipment to cut and program replacement keys and key fobs for most vehicle makes and models. We can meet you in any Southside parking lot or at your home or office.

What's the difference between rekeying and replacing a lock?

Rekeying changes the internal pins so old keys no longer work — it's faster and less expensive than full replacement and uses the same lock hardware. Replacement makes sense when hardware is worn, you want higher security, or you're adding smart lock functionality. We'll recommend the right option when we see your locks.

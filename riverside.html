Frequently Asked Questions — Riverside Locksmith

Can you work on older and historic locks in Riverside homes?

Yes. Riverside and Avondale have many homes with mortise locks, skeleton key hardware, and other period lock types. We work in these neighborhoods regularly and come prepared with the right tools and parts for older hardware.

How quickly can you reach Riverside for an emergency lockout?

For most of Riverside and Avondale, we typically arrive within 15 to 30 minutes for emergency calls. We'll give you a realistic estimate when you call.

Should I replace the original locks on my Riverside historic home?

Not necessarily. In many cases, original hardware can be rekeyed or serviced rather than replaced. Yes. We install smart locks on standard residential doors throughout Riverside. For historic doors with non-standard hardware, we'll assess compatibility and give you options before starting any work.
